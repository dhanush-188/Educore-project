KNOW MORE ABOUT COURSES

MCA

A computer technology-oriented course is much sought after by the software industry for their programming needs. In order to make this course more relevant, students are given courses on languages like C, C#, Java, Python, Data Structures, Operating System, R , DBMS and many more for students to know the basics of computer science.

MBA

Our college gives all students an opportunity for overall development . Our MBA course has carved a niche in the fields of management education over a period of 17 years. Our MBA course has been designed to meet the needs of the modern business world. It teaches about marketing, management, finance, banking law, retail and production management,income tax many more.

MCOM

Master of Commerce is the most sought after under-graduate course in the education field. It is most suitable for those who aim to study for CA, ICWA, CS, MBA etc. It is a wide spectrum course leading to specialization in Finance, Marketing, HR, Auditing, Banking and many more. which have wide employment opportunity.



ADMISSION ELIGIBILITY

MCA

PUC in Science or Commerce or -
A candidate who has passed the 3-year degree in the computer science examination conducted by the National Board of Technical Education shall be eligible for admission in the BCA course.

MBA

PUC in any stream or -
A candidate who has passed the 3-year degree or in the respective stream conducted by the National Board Education shall be eligible for admission in the BBA course.

MCOM

PUC in any stream or -
A candidate who has passed the 3-year degree or in the respective stream conducted by the National Board Education , Two-year JOC , three year diploma students in CP/MOP eligible to get admission directly into third semester or first semester in the BCOM course.
