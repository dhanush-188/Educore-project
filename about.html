INTRODUCTION

Educore College was Established in 2000 with the objective of training students for various competitive exams. Today mere academic excellence is not sufficient to ensure entry into the portals of higher education or employment.

Our objective is to fulfill this lacuna between academic input and real-time requirement,by providing systematic coaching for entrance exams, coaching for various competitive exams conducted by UPSC, KPSC, Indian Railways, Defense Services, Banks, Insurance, MNCs and other organizations.

The Outstanding performance of the college has enabled it to carve a niche for itself in the Malnad region of Karnataka. In 2005, the college started the BBM course affiliated to OXFORD University and since then has stood consistently at the top, bagging university ranks every year

Later in 2010, the college started the M.Com course and our students have done exceedingly well every year. The college started MCA course in 2017 with a full fledged computer lab and add-on software courses, so that the graduates are readily employable after completing their degree.



VISION

Strive to be a center for excellence, inspiring students to bring out their best, to meet the emerging challenges of life and its betterment.To provide quality education to students and promote their holistic development. To create a environment where students can learn and grow, and become the best version of themselves.

MISSION

To unleash hidden potential of the students, grooming their aptitude, skills and knowledge, to enable for better quality of life, by emphasizing on, learning beyond books and performing beyond marks. To advance their interests in Business Management, Commerce, IT and other emerging areas of Education and Employment.
